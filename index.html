About
me

Hi, I'm Naomi, a mathematician and operational research professional based in Manchester, UK.

For the last decade I've used my analytical skills to support decision making in the public and private sector. I've built optimisation, simulation and scenario planning models, analysed vast quantities of data and currently lead a team of Predictive Analysts.

I speak Excel, VBA, SAS, SQL, Simul8, Witness, Tableau and Photoshop. A little rusty in some as I'm currently focusing on R. As you can see from this website, I'm starting to dabble in html, CSS and D3.

My favourite part of any project is figuring out the most effective way to show our customers what we've found, and demonstrate how they can use that information. For reports, I'm a fan of Powerpoint over Word, and Infographic-style 1-pagers.

To that end, I'm on a journey to learn new visualisation methods and improve my graphic design skills, documented here on this website.

Thank you for reading!
